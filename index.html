About

Problem

Everyone wants to maintain healthy relationships, and a large part of these relationships is balanced and meaningful communication. Unfortunately people lead busy lives and can often forget to respond to messages or reach out to their friends. This lack of substantive communication can lead to tension in relationships.

Solution

Our application helps people track and improve the balance, frequency and quality of communication. It analyzes a personâ€™s use of different communication platforms to present data about frequency of contact, communication patterns and overall strength of communication. This allows people to set goals relating to improving these statistics and maintaining healthy communication for more fulfilling relationships.

Choose Contacts

Choose them from various mediums such as Facebook, your contacts, Google Plus, etc.

Set a Goal

Choose to dedicate less, the same, or more effort into your relationship with that person.

Get Reminded

Be reminded to contact someone you have not contacted in a while.
