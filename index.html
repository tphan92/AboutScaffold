About

Problem

Nearly a million jobs are posted to Craigslist every week that range from landscaping to construction. Craigslist and similar platforms offer little protection or assurance to both job workers and job seekers. Other services that do, such as Labor Ready, require strict deadlines and a long hiring process.

Solution

Scaffold is an online communication platform that connects people seeking for temporary work with local employers.

Browse Jobs

Workers can browse current job posts. They can also filter jobs to find a job more tailored to their skill set

Apply

Once a worker has found a job. They can simply apply for the job with one click. After they apply they could choose to fill out an optional blurb about themselves.

Message

After being hired, workers and employers could chat with each other in regards to the upcoming job details.
